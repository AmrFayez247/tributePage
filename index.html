Documentary

A personality that transcended his sport

Guardian boxing correspondent Kevin Mitchell and columnist Gary Younge reflect back on the life and career of the self-proclaimed greatest boxer of all time, Muhammad Ali. The legendary sportsman was a towering figure in his prime; known for his incredible speed, his larger-than-life personality and whose influence was felt far beyond the ring. The boxing icon died in hospital at the age of 74.
